{BIO}GRAPHY

{OUT}SIDERS

We are a MetalElectroDev music band.
We are thrilled to be part of this community.
Using some new technologies, we will drive around Europe to make you hear somethin you've never heard.

OPHELIE

Born in the 90s, Ophélie is a multi-talented girl.
first entered a music university to study what she loved the most : music.
Going through all those boring years, she realized she wanted to get into a much more interesting world, the rock's.
That's how she became one of the group.
Careful, her voice and style will make you shievers.

CHRISTOPHE

Christophe has been playing guitar since he was old enough to hold it.
He honed his musical tastes through independent music radio shows and the BBC session.
While accompanying several artists as a roady on tour, he played in several bands before joining The Outsiders as lead guitarist.

KEVIN

Kevin, is one of the most talented guitarist in the world.
He won many contests all around the world since his childhood, especially in Germany and England, where he used to live many years.
Always here to support the whole group, he is very creative. He composes, writes most of our songs all alone. Even if he is a very hardworking person, he also know how to make people laugh!

AUDREN

Born the 3 December 1993, Audren has been playing since he is 7 years old. First,
he learned piano before falling in love with the bass instrument 3 years later.
Audren, who had never been interested in school, left it at the age of 16 to dedicate himself to the music, despite the disapproving opinion of his parents.
After a few years of practicing, he finally join the Outsiders band and never left it !

GUILLAUME

Guillaume was born on September 30, 1985. A metal lover, he started playing drums at the age of 3.
He tried to learn singing at the age of 10 but he sang like a pan! Currently frenzied drummer for the Outsiders.
He imposes his rhythm in a frenzied way
